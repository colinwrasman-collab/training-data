## LIMITERS & CONSTRAINTS

- **Swim:** Limited pool access + technique gaps. Prioritise drill-based sessions over raw yardage when in the water. Technique efficiency will have more race-day impact than volume at this stage.
- **Bike:** Fitness is the primary gap. Needs progressive aerobic base before any intensity work. Even 2x/week consistency will compound quickly from a low base.
- **Time:** Currently under 6 hrs/week. All planning must work within realistic constraints — no phantom volume. Sessions should be high-signal, low-junk.
- **Gym:** Recently lapsed (2 weeks) after a solid 2–3 month triathlon-specific block. Re-introduce immediately at reduced load. Core and hip stability work directly supports both bike position and run economy — keep it in the plan year-round.
